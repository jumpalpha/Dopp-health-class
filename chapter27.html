Chapter 27
First Aid & Emergencies

Every 40 seconds, someone in the U.S. has a heart attack. 70% of out-of-hospital cardiac arrests happen at home. Knowing CPR, how to stop severe bleeding, how to recognize a stroke, and how to respond to an overdose are skills that could save someone's life — possibly someone you love.

Side Quest — CPR & AED Training
Learn the steps for Hands-Only CPR using American Heart Association guidelines. Practice the compression rate (100–120 per minute) with a pillow. Write a step-by-step guide clear enough that you could teach it to a family member from memory.
